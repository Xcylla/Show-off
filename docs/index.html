The concept


This game is an online multiplayer game that is played with 3 people at a time. The players get to choose their individual element/role, their roles are; Artist, Designer and Engineer. The player stays at this role the entire game! After the players made their choice, they needed to make their last choice of a class/weapon. The weapons of choice are; Bow, Sword and Staff. Each weapon has a different playstyle and different abilities.

Creative media and game technologies


As the name indicates, CMGT will teach you everything you want to know about creative media and game technologies. In the first year you can experiment with different roles and choose the one that is best suited for you. Depending on your preference you will either move in the direction of interactive media in general or specialize in games.
